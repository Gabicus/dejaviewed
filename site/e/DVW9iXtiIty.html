1k ft — Deep-dive / try it

Putting LEDS into used motor oil is just magical! A throwback to 2019 when I was experimenting with this process. I ended up making 3 versions of this work but this round version is my favorite. Sadly used motor oil is very intense on everything it touches so the work didn't last for long. I haven't got the time to find a good alternative unfortunately. Maybe I will in the future. The motor oil just gives it an amazing colour gradient. #engineering #lightart #mechanical #art #light
