Why I’m pivoting from AI Agency to Commodity Broker. I love AI, but running an agency is a constant battle of managing people and churn. In Commodity Brokering, the math is simpler: The pay ceiling doesn’t exist. It’s tied strictly to how much volume you can move. The old guard will tell you this industry is a “closed club.” It used to be impossible to break in unless you spent 20 years in a suit. Not anymore. I’m using the same AI systems I built for my agency to: ✅ Source JIS-certified rebar in Vietnam. ✅ Vet buyers in Japan & globally. ✅ Automate the logistics trail that used to take a team of 10.
