At the center of the visual is a bio-inspired, algorithmically generated 3D web. As a spider’s body approaches the nodes, legs emerge and grow, where the number of legs modulates the pitch and effects—as if they were standing on musical strings. • The two spiders can interact, flirt, or engage in courtship, with their proximity altering the sound’s waveform.
