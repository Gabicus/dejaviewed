Thesis

Prompt engineering is dead. What's replacing it is prompt architecture — the systematic design of context windows, memory layers, retrieval systems, and cognitive scaffolds. The shift from 'write a better prompt' to 'build a better system around the model' is the biggest unlock in practical AI right now.

Why It Matters

Most people are still optimizing individual prompts. The practitioners in these entries have moved past that entirely — they're building systems where the prompt is just one component of a larger architecture. Context management, memory persistence, tool orchestration, and self-correction loops matter more than any single prompt.
